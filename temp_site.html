Who We Are

GEN-Z ADVANCEMENT NETWORK (U) is a non-profit Gen-z led since 2025 organization dedicated to empowering Uganda's refugee and host Gen-Z's population.

We operate with a clear mission: "To equip and connect Gen-Z's with skills, network, and opportunities for sustainable livelihoods and leadership."

Our vision is ambitious yet achievable: "A generation of empowered and innovative Gen-Z's driving social and economic transformation in Uganda."
